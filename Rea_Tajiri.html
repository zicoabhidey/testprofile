Movies

Wisdom Gone Wild

  • Movie Info:In Wisdom Gone Wild, Rea Tajiri explores the lifestory of her mother, Rea Tajiri Noda, during her final sixteen years dealing with dementia. Tajiri explains that her mother, who survived incarceration in an American Concentration Camp during WWII, invents a new name and identity merging the past with the present. The movie wants to deal with the person's perspective rather than the disease.
  • Based on:Rea Tajiri explores with her mother, Rose Tajiri, what it has meant for her to live with dementia
